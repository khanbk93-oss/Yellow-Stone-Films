Yellowstone Films is a Delhi-based production house specialising in large-scale events, documentaries, brand films, and cultural storytelling across India and internationally.

Adept at managing pre- to post-production workflows, on-ground execution, and cross-functional team coordination in fast-paced, multicultural environments. Holds an M.A. in Mass Communication & Filmmaking from AJK MCRC, Jamia Millia Islamia.
